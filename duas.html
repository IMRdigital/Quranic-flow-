🤲 50 Daily Duas

1. Rabbi Zidni Ilma

رَّبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

My Lord, increase me in knowledge.

2. Rabbana Atina Fid-Dunya Hasanah

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً

Grant us goodness in this world and the Hereafter.

3. Hasbunallahu Wa Ni'mal Wakeel

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Allah is sufficient for us.

4. Astaghfirullah

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ

I seek forgiveness from Allah.

5. SubhanAllahi Wa Bihamdihi

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ

Glory and praise belong to Allah.

6. Allahummaghfir Li

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي

O Allah, forgive me.

7. Allahummahdini

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي

O Allah, guide me.

8. Rabbi Ishrah Li Sadri

رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي

My Lord, expand my chest.

9. La Ilaha Illallah

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ

There is no god but Allah.

10. Allahumma Inni As'alukal Jannah

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْجَنَّةَ

O Allah, I ask You for Paradise.

11. Allahumma A'inni Ala Dhikrika

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ

O Allah, help me remember You.

12. Rabbi Yassir Wala Tu'assir

رَبِّ يَسِّرْ وَلَا تُعَسِّرْ

My Lord, make it easy and do not make it difficult.

13. Allahumma Rizqan Tayyiban

اللَّهُمَّ ارْزُقْنِي رِزْقًا طَيِّبًا

O Allah, grant me lawful sustenance.

14. Allahumma Barik Lana

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا

O Allah, bless us.

15. Allahumma Tawaffani Musliman

اللَّهُمَّ تَوَفَّنِي مُسْلِمًا

O Allah, let me die as a Muslim.

16. Allahumma Inni A'udhu Bika Minan Naar

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ النَّارِ

O Allah, protect me from the Fire.

17. Rabbi Habli Min Ladunka Rahmah

رَبِّ هَبْ لِي مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً

My Lord, grant me mercy from Yourself.

18. Allahumma Salli Ala Muhammad ﷺ

اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ

O Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad ﷺ.

19. Subhanallah

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ

Glory be to Allah.

20. Alhamdulillah

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ

All praise belongs to Allah.

1. Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللهِ

Bismillah

Allah ke naam se.

21. After Eating

Alhamdulillahilladhi at'amana wa saqana wa ja'alana minal muslimeen

Allah ka shukr jisne hume khilaya, pilaya aur Musalman banaya.

22. Before Sleeping

Allahumma bismika amutu wa ahya

Ae Allah, tere naam ke saath sota aur jagta hoon.

23. After Waking Up

Alhamdulillahilladhi ahyana ba'da ma amatana wa ilaihin nushur

Allah ka shukr jisne hume maut jaisi neend ke baad zinda kiya.

24. For Knowledge

رَّبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

Rabbi Zidni Ilma

Ae Mere Rab, mere ilm mein izafa farma.

25. Entering Home

Bismillahi walajna wa bismillahi kharajna

Allah ke naam se hum ghar mein dakhil hue.

26. Leaving Home

Bismillahi tawakkaltu alallah

Maine Allah par bharosa kiya.

27. Entering Masjid

Allahumma iftah li abwaba rahmatik

Ae Allah, apni rehmat ke darwaze khol de.

28. Leaving Masjid

Allahumma inni as'aluka min fadlik

Ae Allah, main tera fazl maangta hoon.

29. Entering Bathroom

Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal khubthi wal khabaith

Main buri cheezon se Allah ki panah chahta hoon.

30. Leaving Bathroom

Ghufranak

Ae Allah, main teri maghfirat chahta hoon.

31. For Parents

Rabbir hamhuma kama rabbayani saghira

Mere walidain par reham farma.

32. Seeking Forgiveness

Astaghfirullah

Main Allah se maafi maangta hoon.

33. For Guidance

Ihdinas Siratal Mustaqeem

Hume seedha rasta dikha.

34. For Rizq

Allahummarzuqni rizqan tayyiban

Mujhe halal aur achha rizq ata farma.

35. For Patience

Rabbana afrigh alayna sabran

Hum par sabr nazil farma.

36. For Health

Allahumma afini fi badani

Mujhe sehat aur tandurusti de.

37. For Protection

Hasbunallahu wa ni'mal wakeel

Allah hamare liye kaafi hai.

38. For Success

Rabbi yassir wala tu'assir

Ae Allah, aasani farma.

39. For Good Ending

Allahumma ahsin aqibatana

Hamara anjaam behtareen bana.

40. Tasbeeh

SubhanAllah

Allah paak hai.

41. Praise

Alhamdulillah

Saari tareef Allah ke liye hai.

42. Greatness

Allahu Akbar

Allah sabse bada hai.

43. Tawheed

La ilaha illallah

Allah ke siwa koi mabood nahi.

44. Durood

Allahumma salli ala Muhammad

Ya Allah, Nabi ﷺ par rehmat nazil farma.

45. Seeking Forgiveness

Rabbighfir li

Ae Mere Rab, mujhe maaf farma.

46. For Mercy

Ya Hayyu Ya Qayyum bi rahmatika astagheeth

Ae Zinda aur Qayyam Rabb, teri rehmat ka talabgar hoon.

47. For Jannah

Allahumma inni as'alukal jannah

Ae Allah, main Jannat maangta hoon.

48. Protection from Hell

Allahumma ajirni minan nar

Mujhe Jahannam se bacha.

49. For Wisdom

Rabbi habli hukman

Mujhe hikmat ata farma.

50. For Need

Rabbi inni lima anzalta ilayya min khairin faqeer

Main teri khair ka mohtaj hoon.

51. For Blessings

Allahumma barik lana

Hamare liye barkat ata farma.

52. For Help

Rabbi inni maghloobun fantasir

Main kamzor hoon, meri madad farma.

53. Ease in Work

Rabbi ishrah li sadri

Mera seena khol de aur kaam aasan kar.

54. For Righteous Children

Rabbi habli minas saliheen

Mujhe nek aulaad ata farma.

55. For Well-being

Allahumma inni as'alukal afiyah

Main tujhse afiyat maangta hoon.

56. Against Worry

Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal hammi wal hazan

Main gham aur pareshani se teri panah chahta hoon.

57. Steadfast Faith

Allahumma thabbit qalbi ala deenik

Mere dil ko deen par qayam rakh.

58. Good in This World and Hereafter

Rabbana atina fid dunya hasanah wa fil akhirati hasanah wa qina azaban nar

Duniya aur akhirat ki bhalai ata farma.

59. Repentance

Rabbi inni zalamtu nafsi faghfir li

Maine apne aap par zulm kiya, mujhe maaf farma.

60. Beneficial Knowledge

Allahumma inni as'aluka ilman nafi'a

Mujhe faydemand ilm ata farma.

61. Gratitude

Rabbi awzi'ni an ashkura ni'matak

Mujhe teri nematon ka shukr ada karne ki taufeeq de.

62. Good Ending

Allahumma inni as'aluka husnal khatimah

Mujhe achha anjaam naseeb farma.

63. Allah's Pleasure

Allahumma inni as'aluka ridaka wal jannah

Main teri raza aur jannat maangta hoon.

64. Not Left Alone

Rabbi la tadharnee fardan wa anta khairul wariseen

Mujhe akela na chhod.

65. Trust in Allah

Allahumma inni tawakkaltu alayk

Maine tujh par bharosa kiya.

66. Seeking Mercy

Allahumma inni as'aluka rahmah

Main teri rehmat maangta hoon.

67. Declaration of Faith

Allahumma anta rabbi la ilaha illa anta

Tu hi mera Rab hai, tere siwa koi mabood nahi.

68. Morning Protection

Bismillahil ladhi la yadurru ma'asmihi shay'un

Allah ke naam ke saath jis ke naam se koi nuksan nahi hota.

69. Final Dua

Subhana Rabbika Rabbil izzati amma yasifoon wa salamun alal mursaleen walhamdulillahi rabbil alameen

Pak hai aapka Rab, aur tamam tareef Allah ke liye hai.
