🤲 50 Daily Duas

1. Rabbi Zidni Ilma

رَّبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

My Lord, increase me in knowledge.

2. Rabbana Atina Fid-Dunya Hasanah

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً

Grant us goodness in this world and the Hereafter.

3. Hasbunallahu Wa Ni'mal Wakeel

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Allah is sufficient for us.

4. Astaghfirullah

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ

I seek forgiveness from Allah.

5. SubhanAllahi Wa Bihamdihi

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ

Glory and praise belong to Allah.

6. Allahummaghfir Li

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي

O Allah, forgive me.

7. Allahummahdini

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي

O Allah, guide me.

8. Rabbi Ishrah Li Sadri

رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي

My Lord, expand my chest.

9. La Ilaha Illallah

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ

There is no god but Allah.

10. Allahumma Inni As'alukal Jannah

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْجَنَّةَ

O Allah, I ask You for Paradise.

11. Allahumma A'inni Ala Dhikrika

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ

O Allah, help me remember You.

12. Rabbi Yassir Wala Tu'assir

رَبِّ يَسِّرْ وَلَا تُعَسِّرْ

My Lord, make it easy and do not make it difficult.

13. Allahumma Rizqan Tayyiban

اللَّهُمَّ ارْزُقْنِي رِزْقًا طَيِّبًا

O Allah, grant me lawful sustenance.

14. Allahumma Barik Lana

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا

O Allah, bless us.

15. Allahumma Tawaffani Musliman

اللَّهُمَّ تَوَفَّنِي مُسْلِمًا

O Allah, let me die as a Muslim.

16. Allahumma Inni A'udhu Bika Minan Naar

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ النَّارِ

O Allah, protect me from the Fire.

17. Rabbi Habli Min Ladunka Rahmah

رَبِّ هَبْ لِي مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً

My Lord, grant me mercy from Yourself.

18. Allahumma Salli Ala Muhammad ﷺ

اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ

O Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad ﷺ.

19. Subhanallah

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ

Glory be to Allah.

20. Alhamdulillah

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ

All praise belongs to Allah.
